About Us

FixIT, who are we?

We provide computer service in Warsaw. We diagnose and repair PCs, laptops and tablets of all the most popular brands available on the market. We also offer repair of printers, scanners and multifunction devices and GPS navigation.

We deal with modification and expansion of computer hardware. In our offer, as one of the few services, we also have a computer service at the customer's place (both at companies or institutions, as well as at private individuals).

We offer the best quality service

We provide complex assistance in the IT technology. We employ a team of specialists who are qualified and experienced, which allows us to offer fast and smooth service.

With full commitment we perform all tasks entrusted to us, we also advise the most advantageous solutions for the client while at the same time providing attractive prices for services.
